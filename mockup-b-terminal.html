06 / FAQ

Frequently asked.

QUICK ANSWERS // METHODOLOGY LINKED
What is MEV-Boost?

MEV-Boost is middleware that lets Ethereum validators outsource block construction to specialised builders via relays, in exchange for a higher share of MEV revenue.

What does OFAC compliant mean?

The relay refuses to propagate blocks containing transactions to or from US-sanctioned addresses (e.g. Tornado Cash), effectively censoring them.

Why does this matter?

Credible neutrality is foundational. If most relays filter the same set of transactions, Ethereum's social contract degrades.

How is this measured?

Blocks are compared against a list of OFAC-sanctioned addresses and tagged with the delivering relay (via Relayscan + beacon).

What should validators do?

Configure mev-boost with only neutral relays: Ultra Sound, Aestus, Agnostic, Manifold, BloXroute Max Profit.

Is this on-chain verifiable?

Block contents yes. Relay attribution is from public APIs and may lag a few slots for very recent data.
